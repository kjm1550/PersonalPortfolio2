A Little About Myself

I am a dedicated web developer with a keen eye for detail and a love for innovative solutions. Whether it's coding responsive interfaces or integrating APIs for dynamic content, I am committed to delivering cutting-edge solutions that not only meet, but exceed user expectations. My journey in web development first started in 2017 driven by the endless potential of the web. I taught myself the fundamental of the web while I studding Computer Systems Technology at Illinois State University. After graduation, I got a job at White Oak Technology Group as a web developer, where I built a foundation in my skills and gained experience working with a variety of web technologies. I then moved on to work at Impact Networking as a front-end web developer. This is where my web development journey started to be driven by a passion for crafting engaging, accessible, and dynamic web sites. I continue to push myself and my sites to be the best they can!
